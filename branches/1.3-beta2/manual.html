Movement

  • It's direction based, simply tap in the desired direction
  • Double tap for movement until something interesting happens or you are blocked (g movement)
  • You can zoom in and out with the usual pinching gesture
  • You can pan around. If the view is panned, direction based movement is disabled and clicks are interpreted as direct tile clicks, so the engine will either try to move your char there or interact with the tile
  • You can tap yourself for default action (use stairs if present, pick something up, rest ...)

Shortcuts

  • You can swipe the shortcuts at the bottom to access the next/previous set
  • They usually do what's written on them, so '99.' rests 99 times.
  • 'abc' brings up the keyboard, 'menu' lets you access the main menu (which you undoubtedly already have used or you wouldn'be reading this)
  • There's no ^P (show previous messages). Instead use the main menu (show log)
  • You can customize your shortcuts. Invoke a menu by tapping on a shortcut and holding until it pops up

Stash Management

  • You can swipe in single-item menus (throw and drop) to specify amounts.
